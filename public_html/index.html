Our Service
At Blitz Vision, we believe in the power of innovation to transform the way brands connect with audiences. As a cutting-edge digital out-of-home (DOOH) advertising company, we specialize in delivering dynamic, high-impact digital displays that captivate, engage, and drive real results. We bring your brand’s message to life, ensuring it reaches the right audience at the right time.
01
Digital Billboard Advertising

Kenya's top choice for dynamic DOOH advertising is our digital billboards, strategically located across busy areas, offering flexibility in targeting specific audiences and real-time message delivery.

02
In-Mall Digital Displays

Our in-mall digital displays in Kenya engage shoppers with dynamic content, promoting your brand to active consumers, inspire purchases, and boost brand awareness.

03
Event-Based Advertising

Our event-based digital displays enable brands to engage large audiences at Kenya's top events, inspiring action during key moments with real-time digital capabilities.

About Blitz Vision

Blitz Vision was founded with a single goal in mind: to revolutionize outdoor advertising by harnessing the full potential of digital technology. Our team of industry experts and creative minds is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what’s possible in DOOH, delivering solutions that make brands stand out in a crowded marketplace.

Our company's objective is to provide companies with creative, data-driven digital advertising solutions that improve exposure and engagement levels. Their goal is to transform static settings into dynamic digital experiences, revolutionizing African outdoor advertising. Our core values are:

  • Integrity
  • Innovation
  • Quality
